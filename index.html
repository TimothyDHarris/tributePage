Avatar Aang: The Last Airbender

The boy who saved the world

Here is a timeline of Avatar Aang's life

  • 12 BG - Aang was born an Air Nomad
  • Around 9 BG - Aang is moved to live at the Southern Air Temple
  • Around 6 BG - Aang becomes bonded to his sky-bison (Appa)
  • 0 BG - Aang earns his airbending tattoos and the status of airbending master
  • 0 BG - Aang is told that he is the Avatar and he flees in fear
  • 99 AG - Aang is found frozen in ice by Katara and Sokka near the Souther Water Tribe
  • Winter 99 AG - Aang masters Waterbending as he learns of Sozin's Comet
  • Spring 100 AG - Aang begins his training with Toph to learn Earthbending
  • Late Spring 100 AG - Aang is struck by lightning in the back from Azula when in the Avatar State and is seriously injured
  • Summer 100 AG - Aang is part of the invasion during the Day of the Black Sun
  • Late Summer 100 AG - Aang and Zuko learn the anciet secrets of Firebending from the dragons Ran and Shaw
  • Late Summer 100 AG - Aang meets a lion turtle who teaches him the art of Energybending
  • End of Summer 100 AG - Aang uses Energybending to take away Fireloard Ozai's bending and restores peace to the four nations
  • Around 115 AG - Aang has his three children and works on the founding of Republic City
  • 128 AG - Aang defeats Yakone after his illegal activity (involving Bloodbending) and takes away his bending
  • 152 AG - Aang dies at the biological age of sixty-six due to the stress on his body from the 100 years frozen in ice in the Avatar State
  • After Aang's Death - Aang is memoralized with the creation of Aang Memorial Island in the harbor of Republic City
